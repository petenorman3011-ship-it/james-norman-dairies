Our story
From doorstep to wholesale — a 100 year journey

The Norman family has been delivering fresh dairy to the people of the North East for over a century. What started as a humble doorstep milk round — waking before dawn to ensure every household had fresh milk — has grown into one of the region's most trusted wholesale dairy operations.

Through four generations, the commitment has never changed: fresh, quality dairy, delivered reliably, every single day. The methods have evolved, the scale has grown, but the values remain exactly as they were when it all began.

Our history
A century in the making
🥛
1920s — 1st Generation

The doorstep round begins

The Norman family starts delivering fresh milk door-to-door across local streets in the North East. Up before sunrise every morning, glass bottles clinked on doorsteps before households woke.

🐄
1950s — 2nd Generation

Growing the round

The second generation takes the reins, expanding the milk round to serve more streets and communities. The family builds a reputation for reliability that neighbours pass on by word of mouth.

🚚
1980s — 3rd Generation

From rounds to routes

The third generation modernises the operation — swapping milk floats for vans and beginning to serve local businesses. The first cafés, corner shops and restaurants come on board.

🏢
2000s — 4th Generation

Wholesale expansion

James Norman takes over the family business and drives a major expansion into wholesale. Hotels, care homes, restaurants and supermarkets across the North East join a growing portfolio of customers.

📱
Today

Modern dairy, family values

James Norman Milktech Ltd now serves hundreds of businesses across the region. Ordering is simpler than ever via WhatsApp — but the commitment to fresh, quality dairy delivered on time hasn't changed one bit.
